Weather Website Site Plan

Site Name & Domain

The name of my site is going to be Idaho Nightime Forecasts, or I.N.F. for short. Using google domains I found an available domain name for my site: nightimeforecast.com which costs $12 a year.


Site Purpose

The purpose of my website is to provide a weather forecast specifically focused on accurate nightime predictions. My site is for people who want specific information about the night. This could be people who might want to camp or stargaze or do other outdoor activities at night. Content I want to inculde is the weather, ground temperature for campers, some star and moon information, and alerts about possible dangerous situations.


Logo

Here is my logo, I designed it myself using illustrator and I chose to do a moon because my site has a night theme and it just made sense since it's talking about the weather at night.
