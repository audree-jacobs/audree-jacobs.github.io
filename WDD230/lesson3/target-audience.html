Target Audience

General

Who: Outdoorsy people, hikers, campers, families planning a vaction etc.
Age: 18-60
Technology: probably majority moblie users looking up the conditions on the road or last minute, very few larger screen users
Income: large range of incomes
Motivation: to be prepared, seeking adventure and a fun time



Personas

Scenarios

  • a 16 year old girl wants to know what stars will be out tonight
  • a dad wants to know how cold it will get during his families camping trip next week
  • a gardner wants to know if there was a frost last night
  • a fisherman wants to know how cold the water temperature of the local river gets at night
  • a mom wants to know how much snow will accumulate during the night
