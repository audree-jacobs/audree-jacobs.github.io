Camping in Fish Haven

by Emilio Regino

I've always loved camping in the remote woods of Idaho, but of course, one can't set out on one of these excurssions without heavily preparing beforehand for the possible dangers that one will face in such a harsh climate. Idaho is the heart of bear country and in the spring and summer always remember to pack your bear spray, and make sure to clean up all trash and food from your campsite especialy before going to bed. Eeven during the summer months, as temperatures suddenly drop at night, due to the desert nature of our region, and even on a hot day, you can suddenly find yourself unprepared and spending the night in below freezing temperatures...
