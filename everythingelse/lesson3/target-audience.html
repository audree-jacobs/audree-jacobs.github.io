Target Audience

The target audience for this website are middle aged to older people who are looking for information on the weather. Younger people are more likely to be using an app on their phone unless their looking for weridly specific information, like extended forcasts or astronomical information. Middle aged to older audiences would more likley google weather information than have an app on their phone. w

Persona 1:
Karen Smith, 54, Bakery owner, not very tech savy, is looking to find out what the weather will be like this weekend, as she's planning a picnic with her kids and her sister, Sharon. It's only Sunday and she wants to know the weather for Saturday becasue she likes to plan things in advance. Her main concern is if its going to rain on them during the picnic.

Persona 2:
Phil Berenstein, 78, retired insurance salesman, is going fishing tommorrow morning with some buddies and is wondering what the weather willl be like and also at what time the sun will rise because he wants to leave as early as possible. He only has a landline, and doesn't like to use the internet.

Scenario 1:
Jessica, a sophmore in high school is sneaking out to go star gazing tonight with her boyfreind and wants to know if the grass will be wet. She has her phone's weather app but it doesn't show what the weather was like for teh past few days, only days into the future. She has turned to google to try to find out yesterday's weather in Idaho and if it has rained.

Scenario 2:
James, a 32 year old single accountant has a date tonight and is hoping to get rooftop seating at the restaurant he has reservations at. He's on his computer at work and wants to quickly find out what the weather is like tonight and if it will be comfortable for them to sit outside.

Angelica, a single mother of three very rambunctious boys has just been asked by her seven year old how much annual rainfall they get at their home in preston Idaho.
